HARNESS THE BLOCKCHAIN'S PROMISE

Mantle is a true Blockchain-as-a-Service that is engineered to help companies fast track their adoption of blockchain technology.

HOW MANTLE WORKS

CUSTOM BLOCKCHAIN NETWORK

 Deploy custom blockchain networks through a user friendly Wordpress-like web panel. Mantle lets you focus a maximum of resources and efforts to the top level application that will give a crucial meaning to your network and its tokens.

ENHANCED MANAGEMENT MODULES

 Once your blockchain asset is deploy in a decentralized blockchain, it is just the beginning. Mantle offers you enhanced management modules to let you maximize the potential of your blockchain network such as key management, user accounts, transaction safety and emergency recovery.

CUSTOM UTILITIES

 We also provide you with a customized software suite for users of your new blockchain asset such as a block explorer, wallet service, mobile wallet and decentralized exchange to make sure your new asset can be used with ease.

WHY MANTLE

Blockchain assets have been around for a while now yet, the technology and its benefits are not quite accessible for the public since corporations face major impediments when it comes to developing a blockchain based commercial application. With lower development costs, mitigated risks around keys and the enhanced management modules, that makes for the first time, a blockchain system foolproof. Mantle definitely aims to propel the adoption of blockchain assets by the industry.
